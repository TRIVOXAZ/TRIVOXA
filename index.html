Schemes

Enroll in all major Government of India financial inclusion and social security schemes right here. No need to visit a main bank branch.

PMJDY
Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana

Financial Inclusion for Every Household

Launched in 2014 to ensure every household has access to a basic savings account. Comes with zero minimum balance, a free RuPay debit card with ₹2 lakh accident insurance cover, overdraft facility up to ₹10,000, and direct transfer of all government subsidies (DBT). Over 56 crore accounts opened nationwide.

Zero Balance Account Free RuPay Card DBT Benefits
☂️
PMJJBY
Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

Life Insurance for All Citizens

Government-backed life insurance launched on 9th May 2015. Provides ₹2 lakh life cover in case of death due to any reason. Annual premium is only ₹436/year, auto-debited from your savings account. Eligible age: 18 to 50 years. Policy renews every year from 1st June to 31st May. Over 23 crore people enrolled.

₹2 Lakh Life Cover ₹436 / Year Age 18–50 Years
🛡️
PMSBY
Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana

Accidental Death & Disability Cover

Personal accident insurance launched on 9th May 2015. Covers accidental death & total permanent disability: ₹2 lakh. Partial permanent disability: ₹1 lakh. Annual premium just ₹20/year — less than ₹2 per month. Auto-debited from savings account. Eligible for account holders aged 18 to 70 years. Over 51 crore enrolled.

₹2 Lakh Accident Cover Only ₹20 / Year Age 18–70 Years
👴
APY
Atal Pension Yojana

Guaranteed Monthly Pension After Retirement

Government pension scheme launched on 9th May 2015 to create a social security system for all Indians — especially unorganized sector workers. Subscribers receive guaranteed monthly pension of ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 after age 60. Eligible for savings account holders aged 18 to 40 years who are not income tax payers. Administered by PFRDA.

₹1,000–₹5,000/Month Pension After 60 Age 18–40 Years
🌸
SSY
Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Savings Scheme for Your Daughter's Future

Government savings scheme under "Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao" initiative. Parents or guardians can open account for a girl child below 10 years. Current interest rate: 8.2% per annum (compounded) — one of the highest among all government schemes. Min deposit: ₹250/year, Max: ₹1.5 lakh/year. Deposits for 15 years; matures at age 21. Full tax exemption under Section 80C.

8.2% Interest p.a. Girl Child Below 10 Tax Free Returns
